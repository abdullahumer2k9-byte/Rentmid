Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how RentMid ("we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when landlords, property managers, and tenants use our property management platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect account information (name, email, company), property and unit information, payment and billing details processed through our payment provider, communications sent through the platform, and maintenance requests including any photos or video submitted by tenants.

3. Tenant Screening Data

Where a landlord uses our tenant screening integration, background check, credit check, and eviction history information is collected and processed by our third-party screening provider under its own privacy terms, and shared with the requesting landlord for application review.

4. How We Use Information

Information is used to operate the platform: processing rent payments, tracking maintenance requests, managing leases and renewal reminders, generating financial reports, and sending account, billing, and property-related notifications.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We share information with service providers who help us operate the platform (such as payment processors and screening providers), and as required by law.

6. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information stored on the platform. No system is completely secure, and this section should be expanded with specifics once RentMid's infrastructure is finalized.

7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the platform and to comply with legal, tax, and recordkeeping obligations, including state-specific requirements for lease and tenant records.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain personal information. This section must be tailored to the specific rights available in RentMid's launch state before publication.

9. Children's Privacy

RentMid is intended for business use by adults managing rental properties and is not directed at children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated to account holders.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to us through the contact page.
