Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Template notice: this is a starting draft, not finished legal advice. RentMid handles tenant financial and background-check data, which carries real regulatory obligations (state privacy law, FCRA if screening data is involved, and more). Have a licensed attorney review and complete this before publishing it live.

1. Who this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains how RentMid, Inc. ("RentMid," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when property managers and landlords ("Customers") use our platform, and when tenants interact with a Customer's account (for example, through the tenant portal). Customers are responsible for their own use of tenant data and for their own notices to tenants where required by law.

2. Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as account and billing details, property and unit records, lease documents, and messages sent through the platform. We also collect information tenants provide through rental applications and the tenant portal, including contact details, payment information, and maintenance request content (including uploaded photos or video). Screening results (background, credit, and eviction history) are provided by our third-party screening partners and stored on your account.

We also collect limited technical information automatically — device and browser type, IP address, and usage data — to keep the service secure and working correctly.

3. How we use information

  • To operate the core features of the platform: rent collection, lease and document storage, maintenance tracking, messaging, reporting, and screening workflows.
  • To send account, billing, and service notifications.
  • To maintain security, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms of Service.
  • To improve the product, using aggregated or de-identified data wherever possible.

We do not sell personal information.

4. How information is shared

We share information with service providers who help us operate the platform — for example, payment processors for ACH and card transactions, and screening partners (such as TransUnion SmartMove or similar providers) for background and credit checks. These providers are only permitted to use the data to perform the service we've engaged them for. We may also disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or valid legal process.

5. Data security

We use industry-standard safeguards — including encryption in transit, access controls, and regular security review — to protect data on the platform. No system is perfectly secure, and we encourage Customers to use strong, unique passwords and to report any suspected unauthorized access immediately.

6. Data retention

We retain account and tenant data for as long as an account is active, and for a limited period afterward as required for legal, tax, or dispute-resolution purposes. Customers can request deletion of specific records subject to those legal retention requirements.

7. Your rights

Depending on your state, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information, and to opt out of certain uses. Requests can be sent to the contact below; we will verify the request and respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

8. Children's privacy

RentMid is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Changes to this policy

We'll post any material changes here and update the "last updated" date above. Continued use of RentMid after a change means you accept the updated policy.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@rentmid.com.
