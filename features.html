RENT COLLECTION & PAYMENTS

Get paid on time, without the follow-up

Rent moves by ACH bank transfer or card, with reminders sent automatically before each due date so tenants rarely need a nudge.

  • Automated ACH and card collection
  • Reminders before rent is due
  • Late flagging, fee automation, and follow-up workflows
  • Full payment history, per tenant and per property
MAINTENANCE REQUEST TRACKING

Every request, tracked from photo to close

Tenants file requests with photos or video from the portal. Managers assign, prioritize, and track them on a single dashboard.

  • Tenant-facing portal with photo/video upload
  • Manager dashboard to assign and prioritize
  • Automated status updates back to the tenant
  • Timestamped history for liability protection
LEASE & DOCUMENT MANAGEMENT

Every lease, tracked and never missed

Digital lease storage with renewal-date tracking and automated reminders, plus secure storage for signed leases, applications, and correspondence.

  • Renewal-date tracking with automated reminders
  • State-aware lease templates and clauses
  • Secure storage for leases, applications, and ID verification

TENANT COMMUNICATION PORTAL

One thread, not five apps

A centralized portal replaces scattered texts and emails, with automated notices for rent, maintenance, renewals, and announcements.

  • Centralized messaging per tenant
  • Automated rent-due and lease-renewal notices
  • General announcements to a whole property
FINANCIAL REPORTING

Reporting that's ready for tax season

Income and expense reporting per property and across the portfolio, exportable for accounting and Schedule E preparation, with simple dashboards for occupancy and collections.

  • Per-property and portfolio-level reports
  • Exportable for accounting and tax filing
  • Occupancy rate and rent collected vs. outstanding
TENANT SCREENING INTEGRATION

Screen applicants without leaving the app

Background, credit, and eviction-history screening through a third-party integration (such as TransUnion SmartMove or similar), paired with digital applications and an automated screening workflow.

  • Background, credit, and eviction history checks
  • Digital rental applications
  • Fair Housing Act–aware application workflow
