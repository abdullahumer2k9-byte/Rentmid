Everything, in detail

One system for the whole day-to-day.

Scroll through the six jobs RentMid handles, then see the dashboard where they come together.

Rent collection

Rent Collection & Payments

Automated rent collection via ACH bank transfer or card, with reminders sent before every due date. Late payments are flagged automatically, late fees apply themselves, and follow-up workflows chase down what's owed — so managers aren't the ones sending the awkward text.

Every tenant has a full, searchable payment history, viewable per unit or rolled up across the whole property.

Leases & documents

Lease & Document Management

Leases live in one digital library with renewal dates tracked automatically and reminders sent well before expiration — no more finding out a lease lapsed after the fact.

Lease templates and clauses are state-aware, starting with your launch state, and every signed lease, application, ID, and piece of correspondence has a secure home.

Maintenance

Maintenance Request Tracking

Tenants submit requests through a portal, with photo or video attached, so managers see the problem before showing up. A manager-facing dashboard makes it easy to assign, prioritize, and track status across every open ticket.

Status updates go back to the tenant automatically, and every request keeps a timestamped history — useful documentation if a dispute ever comes up.

Tenant portal

Tenant Communication Portal

A single, centralized thread replaces the scattered mix of texts, emails, and phone calls most managers juggle across dozens of tenants.

Automated notifications cover the routine stuff — rent due, maintenance updates, lease renewals, general announcements — so tenants stay informed without a manager having to remember to tell them.

Financial reporting

Financial Reporting

Income and expenses roll up per property and across the whole portfolio, with reports built to export cleanly for accounting, tax filing, and Schedule E preparation.

Simple dashboards show occupancy rate, rent collected versus outstanding, and maintenance spend at a glance — the numbers a manager actually checks day to day.

Screening

Tenant Screening Integration

Background checks, credit checks, and eviction history run through third-party integrations such as TransUnion SmartMove or similar providers.

Digital rental applications feed straight into an automated screening workflow, so a prospective tenant can apply and get screened without a manager chasing down paperwork.

Launch-state specific

Built around the law, not around a generic checklist.

Landlord-tenant law is set at the state level in the U.S. — and sometimes the city level. Security deposit limits, eviction procedures, and rent stabilization rules can all differ depending on where a property sits. RentMid is designed to expand state by state rather than claim nationwide compliance on day one.

  • State-specific security deposit limits and return timelines
  • Fair Housing Act–aware application and screening workflows
  • Local rent control / rent stabilization tracking in applicable cities

Why not claim nationwide compliance?

Because it wouldn't be true. Rules for deposits, notices, and evictions vary by state and sometimes by city. RentMid launches deep in one state's rules first, then expands — rather than shipping a shallow, one-size-fits-all compliance layer.
