Features

Everything a 20–200 unit operation actually needs

No bundled modules for commercial leasing, no enterprise workflow builder. Six systems, built for the way mid-sized landlords actually run their portfolios.

Rent collection

Get paid on time, automatically

Tenants pay by ACH bank transfer or card. RentMid sends reminders before the due date, flags late payments the moment they happen, and applies late fees according to your lease terms — no manual chasing.

  • Automated ACH and card collection
  • Reminders before due dates, follow-up workflows after
  • Late payment flagging and late-fee automation
  • Full payment history, per tenant and per property

Every lease, tracked and renewing on schedule

Maintenance

From tenant request to closed ticket

Tenants submit requests with photos or video straight from the portal. You assign, prioritize, and track status from one dashboard, and every update — timestamped — is logged automatically, which matters as much for liability protection as it does for keeping tenants informed.

  • Tenant-facing portal with photo/video upload
  • Manager dashboard to assign and prioritize
  • Automated status updates back to the tenant
  • Timestamped history for liability protection

Tenant communication

One thread, not a dozen text chains

Every conversation with a tenant lives in one place, replacing scattered texts and emails. Rent due notices, maintenance updates, lease renewals, and general announcements all go out automatically, so nothing depends on you remembering to send it.

  • Centralized messaging, per tenant
  • Automated rent-due and maintenance notifications
  • Lease renewal and announcement broadcasts
  • Full message history if a dispute ever comes up

Tenant screening

Screen every applicant the same way, every time

Background checks, credit checks, and eviction history run through integrated third-party screening (like TransUnion SmartMove), attached to a digital rental application with an automated workflow — so criteria stay consistent across every unit and every applicant.

  • Background, credit, and eviction history screening
  • Digital rental applications
  • Automated screening workflow
  • Fair Housing–aware application process

State & local compliance

Compliance that expands state by state, honestly

Landlord-tenant law is set at the state — and sometimes city — level in the US, so RentMid doesn't claim nationwide compliance on day one. At launch, RentMid ships with security deposit limits and return timelines, Fair Housing–aware application and screening workflows, and rent control / rent stabilization tracking in applicable cities, all specific to the launch state, with more states added as the product expands.
