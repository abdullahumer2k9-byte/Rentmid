The gap in the middle

Two kinds of software. Neither built for you.

Consumer tools stop scaling around 10 units. Enterprise platforms assume 500+. RentMid is built for what sits in between.

Consumer apps

Built for 1–10 units

Fine for a landlord with a few doors, but the workflows, reporting, and screening tools break down fast once a portfolio grows past a handful of properties.

RentMid

Built for 20–200 units

Full rent collection, maintenance, leasing, and compliance workflows — priced and scaled for a mid-sized operation, not a portfolio ten times its size.

Enterprise platforms

Built for 500+ units

Yardi, AppFolio, and RealPage are powerful, but come with enterprise pricing, sales calls, and onboarding complexity sized for portfolios far larger than yours.

Everything in one place

One system, not six spreadsheets.

Rent Collection & Payments

ACH and card payments, automatic reminders, and late-fee workflows — with full payment history per tenant and property.

Lease & Document Management

Digital lease storage with renewal tracking, state-aware templates, and secure storage for applications and correspondence.

Maintenance Request Tracking

Tenant-facing requests with photo upload, a manager dashboard to assign and track, and a timestamped history for liability protection.

Tenant Communication Portal

One centralized thread with every tenant, replacing scattered texts and emails — plus automated rent, maintenance, and renewal notices.

Financial Reporting

Per-property and portfolio-level income and expense reports, exportable for accounting, tax filing, and Schedule E preparation.

Tenant Screening Integration

Background, credit, and eviction-history screening through third-party providers, with digital applications and automated workflow.
