Billing questions

Frequently asked

How is my monthly bill calculated?+

Your bill is your total unit count multiplied by your tier's per-unit rate, subject to that tier's monthly minimum. If your unit count crosses into a new tier, the new tier's rate applies going forward.

What counts as a "unit"?+

Any individually leasable space you manage in RentMid — an apartment, a single-family rental, or a commercial suite each count as one unit, whether currently occupied or vacant.

Are there setup or onboarding fees?+

No. Pricing is per-unit, per-month only. Larger Pro-tier portfolios can request custom onboarding assistance, but it isn't required to get started.

Can I change tiers as my portfolio grows or shrinks?+

Yes. Your tier is based on your current unit count, so your monthly bill adjusts automatically as you add or remove units — no need to change plans manually.

Is there a long-term contract?+

No. RentMid bills month to month at every tier, without the multi-year contracts common on enterprise platforms.
