Billing FAQ

Questions about pricing

What counts as a "unit"?+

Any individually leasable space you manage in RentMid — an apartment, a single-family rental, a duplex side, or a room in a shared unit if you lease it separately. Vacant units you're actively managing still count; units you've fully retired from your portfolio don't.

How is my monthly bill calculated?+

Your unit count is multiplied by your tier's per-unit rate, with a plan minimum applied if that total falls below it. If you're on Growth with 80 units, for example, that's 80 × $1.99 = $159.20/month.

What happens when I cross a tier boundary?+

Your plan updates automatically to the tier matching your unit count at your next billing cycle — no re-signing, no sales call, no downtime. You can also request an immediate tier change from your account settings.

Is there a setup or onboarding fee?+

No, on Starter and Growth. Pro includes an optional dedicated onboarding call at no extra cost, and Enterprise onboarding is scoped as part of your custom agreement.

Do I need to sign a contract?+

Starter, Growth, and Pro are month-to-month — cancel anytime. Enterprise customers can choose a month-to-month or annual arrangement depending on what works for their organization.

Is there a free trial?+

Yes. Every new account gets a full-featured trial period before your first bill, so you can run RentMid against your actual rent roll before you commit to anything.

What if I manage units in more than one state?+

RentMid launches with full compliance support for one state and expands state by state. If you manage units outside our current coverage, our team will tell you exactly what's supported today before you sign up — nothing is silently assumed to be compliant.
