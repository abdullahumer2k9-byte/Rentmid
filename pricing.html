Billing FAQ

Questions about how billing works

How is my monthly price calculated?

Pricing is per unit, per month. Multiply your unit count by the rate for your tier (Starter, Growth, or Pro) — if that comes out under the tier's monthly minimum, the minimum applies instead.

What happens if my unit count changes?

Your bill is based on the units you currently manage in the system. As your portfolio grows or shrinks, your tier and monthly cost adjust with it — you're never locked into a size you've outgrown.

Are all features included at every tier?

Yes. Rent collection, maintenance tracking, lease management, the tenant portal, financial reporting, and screening integration are part of every plan — pricing scales with unit count, not feature access.

Is there a setup fee or contract?

No. RentMid is billed monthly per unit with no onboarding fees and no long-term contract, unlike typical enterprise platform agreements.

How does Pro tier pricing work for 150–200+ units?

Pro starts at $2.89 per unit per month with a custom minimum based on portfolio specifics. Contact us for an exact quote for your unit count.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes — reach out through the contact page and we'll get you set up. RentMid is pre-launch and onboarding early-access landlords in our first launch state.
